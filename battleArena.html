Battle Arena (Avardgaah)

An original online PvP game, inspired from Archero in it's core mechanics, but with a totally different (and in my mind, more better) experince.

Project Overview

At Part Games, we all were in love with MOBA games, each with thousands of hours of playtime in DOTA 2 and League of Legends. Our ultimate wish was to create a MOBA game for PC. As our first step towards that dream, we started to develop Battle Arena game.
